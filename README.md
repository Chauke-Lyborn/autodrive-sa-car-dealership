# AutoDrive SA - Car Dealership Website

## Overview
AutoDrive SA is a modern, fully responsive car dealership website designed for a
premium automotive platform connecting South Africans with reliable dealerships
and quality pre-owned vehicles.  

This project was developed as part of my web development portfolio to demonstrate
strong frontend development skills, clean UI/UX design, and responsive layouts.

## Live Demo
[View Live Website](https://chauke-lyborn.github.io/autodrive-sa-car-dealership/)

---

## Project Overview
AutoDrive SA provides an engaging and user-friendly experience for potential car
buyers. The website consists of the following pages:

- Home Page: Hero section with call-to-action, featured vehicles, category slider,
  and customer testimonials
- About Page: Company information, team profiles, brand comparisons, and timeline
- Contact Page: Interactive contact form with validation, map location, and
  multiple contact options

---

## Key Features
- ✅ *Fully Responsive Design* - Works perfectly on all devices (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- ✅ *Interactive Car Slider* - Auto-rotating car category showcase with manual controls
- ✅ *Featured Vehicles Grid* - Six premium vehicles with detailed specifications
- ✅ *Contact Form with Validation* - Real-time form validation and submission feedback
- ✅ *Google Maps Integration* - Interactive location map
- ✅ *Testimonial Slider* - Customer reviews with automatic rotation
- ✅ *Modern UI/UX* - Dark theme with red accent colors for premium feel
- ✅ *Smooth Animations* - Scroll animations, hover effects, and transitions
- ✅ *Cross-browser Compatible* - Tested on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge
